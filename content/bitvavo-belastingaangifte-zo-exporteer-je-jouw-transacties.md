### Exporting Your Bitvavo Transactions

To start, log into your Bitvavo account. Navigate to the 'Account' section where you'll find an option labeled 'Reports'. Here, you can generate a report of your transaction history. Choose the time period for which you want to export data. It’s usually advisable to select the entire fiscal year to simplify your tax calculations.

After selecting the timeframe, download the report in CSV format. This file will include essential details such as transaction dates, amounts, and crypto assets involved. Having this document handy will streamline your tax filing process significantly.

### Calculating Taxable Income

In the Netherlands, cryptocurrency is taxed under Box 3, which pertains to savings and investments. As of 2026, the Dutch Box 3 tax rate stands at **36%**. The government assumes a deemed return on your crypto assets of **6.00%**. This means that, regardless of your actual gains, your assets are taxed based on this preset return rate.

#### Example Calculation:

Suppose you hold €100,000 worth of cryptocurrency. The deemed return is calculated as follows:

- **Deemed Return**: €100,000 x 6.00% = €6,000
- **Taxable Income**: €6,000
- **Tax Owed**: €6,000 x 36% = €2,160

Remember, there's a tax-free allowance of **€59,357** (or **€118,714** for fiscal partners), which reduces your taxable base. If your assets fall below this threshold, you may not owe any tax.
