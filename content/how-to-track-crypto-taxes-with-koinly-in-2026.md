## Generating Your Crypto Tax Report

### Step 4: Choose Your Accounting Method

<a href='https://koinly.io' target='_blank' rel='noopener nofollow sponsored'>Koinly</a> allows you to select your preferred accounting method, which determines how your capital gains and losses are calculated. Common methods include:

- **FIFO (First In, First Out)**: The oldest assets are sold first, which may result in higher capital gains if prices have increased over time.

- **LIFO (Last In, First Out)**: The most recently acquired assets are sold first, which can be beneficial in a falling market.

- **HIFO (Highest In, First Out)**: The assets with the highest cost are sold first, potentially reducing taxable gains.

Selecting the right method can have a significant impact on your tax liability, so consider consulting a tax professional if you're unsure.
